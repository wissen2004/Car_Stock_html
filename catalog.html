Catalog of cars

All the performance and functionality you need with zero direct emissions.

Volkswagen e-Lavida is a European C—class sedan, which in the third generation shows a significant step forward in terms of exterior style, quality and luxury of the interior compared to its predecessor.

The seventh-generation Volkswagen Jetta sedan was unveiled at the North American International Auto Show in Detroit in January 2018.

Volkswagen Passat 2020 model year received a slightly modified design and new items in terms of technology. The optics, bumpers and front grille have been updated, although all of them have generally retained the same style.

The Volkswagen Phaeton of the first generation has been produced since 2002. Phaeton is a representative of the premium segment, its luxurious interior is equipped, to choose from, with a solid rear sofa or two separate rear seats.

Volkswagen Passat CC is a four—door "comfort coupe" built on the basis of the sixth-generation Passat. The model debuted in early 2008. The model differs from the basic Passat not only in forms that openly speak about the sporty character of the car, but also in a higher level of equipment.

Volkswagen Saveiro passenger pickup truck, sold in South American markets, is based on the Gol/Pointer model, which is popular in these countries. The fifth generation of the car has been produced in Brazil since 2009, in 2013 the model was restyled.

Compact crossover Taos in the product hierarchy of the Volkswagen brand will stand a step below Tiguan and will compete with such market hits as Hyundai Creta, Kia Seltos and Renault Duster.

The premiere of the restyled Terramont took place in February 2020 in the USA, where it is called Atlas. From the outside of the car of the previous model, it differs in the modified design of the front part, as well as another rear bumper.

The Volkswagen Tiguan of the second generation has been produced since 2016. The upgraded version was introduced in 2020. The design of the front and rear parts of the body has been changed.
